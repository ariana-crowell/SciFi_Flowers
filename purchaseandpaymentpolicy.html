SciFi Flowers Purchase and Payment Policy

At SciFi Flowers, we are committed to providing you with exceptional floral arrangements and a smooth, secure shopping experience. Our Purchase and Payment Policy outlines the terms and conditions related to ordering, payments, and delivery. By placing an order with us, you agree to the following policies:

  1. Ordering Process
    • Placing an Order: To place an order with SciFi Flowers, simply browse our online shop and select the items you wish to purchase. Once you've chosen your arrangement(s), you can proceed to checkout where you'll provide shipping details and payment information.
    • Order Confirmation: After submitting your order, you will receive an email confirmation acknowledging that we’ve received your order. Please verify all details in this confirmation and contact us immediately if there are any errors.
    • Customization: If you have specific requests or need to customize your order (e.g., particular flowers, colors, or arrangements), please contact us directly before placing your order or note the customization request during checkout. While we strive to fulfill all special requests, availability may depend on current stock.
    • Availability: All items are subject to availability. If a flower or item in your order is unavailable, we will contact you to discuss alternatives or arrange a refund.
  2. Pricing
    • Pricing Information: Prices for all products are listed in USD and include applicable taxes. Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on the delivery location and selected delivery method.
    • Promotions and Discounts: We occasionally offer promotions or discounts. These will be clearly stated on the website, and any applicable codes must be entered during the checkout process.
  3. Payment Methods
    • Accepted Payment Methods: SciFi Flowers accepts the following payment methods:
      • Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
      • PayPal
      • Apple Pay and Google Pay
      • Other secure payment methods as indicated during checkout
    • Payment Security: All payment transactions are processed securely through encrypted payment gateways. We do not store any of your payment details on our servers, ensuring your privacy and protection.
    • Payment Authorization: Your payment will be authorized at the time of purchase, and your order will be processed once the payment is successfully verified.
  4. Order Modifications and Cancellations
    • Modifications: If you need to modify your order (e.g., change delivery address, date, or flower type), please contact us as soon as possible. Once your order has been processed or shipped, modifications may not be possible.
    • Cancellations: If you wish to cancel your order, please do so withing 24 hours of placing the order. Orders cancelled after this time may not be refunded due to our commitment to timely processing and flower sourcing.
  5. Delivery and Shipping
    • Delivery Timeframes: We offer several delivery options, including same-day delivery for certain locations. Delivery times vary depending on your location, chosen delivery method, and stock availability. Delivery dates will be confirmed at checkout.
    • Delivery Fees: Shipping fees are calculated based on the delivery address and chosen shipping method. We provide options for standard, expedited, and express delivery.
    • Delivery Address: It is your responsibility to provide an accurate and complete delivery address. SciFi Flowers cannot be held liable for non-delivery due to incorrect or incomplete address information.
    • Failed Delivery: If no one is available to receive the delivery, our delivery team will make one additional attempt. If the second attempt fails, we will contact you for alternate arrangements or to reschedule delivery. A redelivery fee may apply.
  6. Returns and Refunds
    • Return Policy: Due to the perishable nature of our products, we do not accept returns or exchanges. However, if you are not satisfied with your order, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your flowers, and we will do our best to resolve any issues, such as providing a replacement or offering a store credit.
    • Refunds: Refunds will only be processed if there was an error with your order (e.g., incorrect flowers delivered, damaged items). Refunds will be issued to the original payment method, and processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

    9. SciFi Flowers reserves the right to modify or update this Purchase and Payment Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the most recent update will be clearly noted. Please review this policy periodically to stay informed of any changes.
